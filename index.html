Feedback formatter

This form is for converting the output from one line of Google forms into paragraphs of text. This can then be pasted into the feedback box in Turnitin Feedback Centre. The questions in the Google Form should be of the Checkbox type. For compiling a sentence from multiple choices, a hyphen at the end of a line will be converted here to a space. Several other punctuation marks at the end of an item (, ; : ! and ?) will be retained by this formatter. Past your text in here, click on format, then click on cut to clipboard. Switch to Turnitin and paste the clipboard into the text comments box. Turnitin limits this to 5,000 characters. Paste will simply not work for more than it allows, and Turnitin generates no error message. Also, it seems to have an odd way of counting characters, so you may have to edit your text down to below 5,000. There is a character counter below to help you.
